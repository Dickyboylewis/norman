ABOUT WHITE RED ARCHITECTS
===========================

White Red is a UK-based architecture and design practice.

The sales/BD team consists of three directors:
- Dicky Lewis — Director
- Joe Haire — Director  
- Jesus Jimenez — Director

They handle their own business development and prospecting alongside their architecture work. They are not full-time salespeople — they are architects who also sell.

Key sectors White Red operates in:
- Commercial office fit-outs and refurbishments
- Residential developments
- Mixed-use schemes
- Workplace design and strategy

The CRM is managed through Notion and leads are tracked in Monday.com.

Typical deal sizes range from £30,000 to £300,000+ in architectural fees.

The sales cycle is usually 2-6 months from first contact to appointment to proposal to winning the project.
